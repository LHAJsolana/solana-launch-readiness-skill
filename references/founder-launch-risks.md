# Founder Launch Risks

Use this reference when helping founders prepare launch materials and risk disclosures.

## Launch Scope Risk

Founders often describe a broad vision while only a narrow demo is live. Keep launch copy precise:

- What is live today.
- What is experimental.
- What is on devnet.
- What is planned later.

## Token Communication Risk

Token launches need extra care:

- Explain supply, decimals, mint, authorities, vesting, and treasury controls.
- Avoid price targets, guaranteed returns, and fake urgency.
- Publish official mint and pool links carefully.
- Warn about impostor tokens and fake support accounts.

## Ecosystem Claim Risk

Do not invent or exaggerate:

- Partnerships.
- Grants.
- Audits.
- TVL.
- Users.
- Revenue.
- Exchange or aggregator support.

## Support Risk

Before public launch, define:

- Support channel.
- Expected response time.
- Escalation owner.
- Incident update channel.
- FAQ for failed transactions, wallet issues, wrong links, and impostors.

## Demo Risk

Demo assets should not expose:

- Private keys or seed phrases.
- Admin dashboards with destructive controls.
- Secret environment variables.
- Undisclosed user data.
- Internal wallets that can be targeted.
